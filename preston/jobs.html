Full Stack Software Engineer

Job Type: Full-time
Salary Range: $90,000 - $120,000
Position Reference Number: SOF23
Location: Melbourne
Applicant Reporting to: Software Design Team

Brief Description of the Job:

This is a superb role for a seasoned software engineer - you will use your technical skills to design and develop full-stack data-intensive web applications. You are passionate about providing technical leadership and mentoring others to follow the best software engineering practice.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Collaborate with data scientists, engineers, business analysts and product owners to deliver data insights to customers
  • Liaise with business stakeholders and customers to sense check analysis results and workshop solutions.
  • Utilise tools and practices to build, verify and deploy solutions in the most efficient ways, enhancing team capabilities
  • Implement a culture within the team, that encourages best practices around reviews, security, quality and documentation and improve the developer experience
  • Own the development lifecycle (develop, test, deploy) for software engineers by providing consistent guidance on standard, best practices, and design patterns
  • Enable continuous delivery practices to increase delivery speed
  • Provide ongoing support for platforms as required e.g. problem and incident management
  • Collaborate with other teams to explore new business initiatives
  • Contribute to software engineering communities, meetups and conferences to promote technology development culture and practices

Required Qualification(s), Skills and Knowledge (Essential):

  1. Bachelor of Software Engineering
  2. A minimum of 3 years as a full stack developer
  3. Hands-on knowledge and experience with end-to-end data and software product development and delivery including building data pipeline, restful API, and Web application using C#, Python, and cloud technology
  4. Thorough understanding of software development techniques such as Object-Oriented design, Design Pattern, Test-Driven development, and Continuous Delivery
  5. Demonstrated experience in frontend development (React)
  6. Creative technical problem solving and excellent debugging skills

Qualification(s), Skills and Knowledge (Preferable):

  1. Exposure to micro-service architecture
  2. Excellent Git source control skills
  3. Ability to provide leadership in designing effective solution and its implementation
  4. Ability to share knowledge and upskill other team members
  5. Ability to innovate and adapt to latest development in software engineering and bring the best practice to the team
  6. Ability to balance the business and technology requirements and optimise the solution through the trade-off between the requirements

Benefits:

  • Free Breakfast and Lunch provided by us! (Halal/Vegan Options also available!)
  • Free parking!
  • If chosen to be the employee of the month, you may also win gift cards (E.g. $150 Apple Gift Card).
  • Free tickets to a movie theatre, sports game, concert, etc.
  • Public Transportation and Local Shopping Centre located within 1.5 kilometres from our office.
  • On-going training and one-on-one meetings regarding employee performance.

Big Data Analyst

Job Type: Full-Time
Salary Range: $90k - $140k
Position Reference Number: DaT40
Location: Melbourne
Applicant Reporting to: Development Team

Brief Description of the Job:

In this position, you will be collecting, analysing, visualising and communicating big data sets from our users and the market, to develop key insights into how our products are used, and to help shape the direction of our development team and business as a whole.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Mining, organising and visualising big data sets to be presented as information to the development and business teams.
  • Identifying patterns and trends within big data sets to gain insight on the business' trajectory, and the trajectory of the market as a whole.
  • Maintaining company data sets to ensure data is relevant, up-to-date, and accurate for analysis.
  • Collaborate with team members across the company to interpret relevant information from the data that is collected.
  • Maintaining areas of our code base related to data set mining and storage, debugging and fixing errors in these areas and potentially improving them.

Required Qualification(s), Skills and Knowledge (Essential):

  1. Bachelor of Data Analytics OR Bachelor of Data Science.
  2. Minimum of 3 years experience in a previous Data Analytical Job.
  3. Successful Applicant must be familiar with Python and SQL (programming language).
  4. Needs to have acceptable knowledge in Data/Statistics and Mathematics. The applicant should also have knowledge of Calculus and Linear Algebra.
  5. In terms of skill, the applicant is expected to be a highly motivated individual, as well as always beginning a new day with a smile.
  6. Data Visualization is needed. Applicants should be able to comprehend the data which is presented in front of them.

Qualification(s), Skills and Knowledge (Preferable):

  1. We would appreciate a team-worker, or someone who is able to lead a team full of data analysts to always achieve the best outcome.
  2. Fluent Communication among peers is highly recommended.
  3. Knowledge in Microsoft Excel will be a bonus! (Though not compulsory)
  4. We would strongly prefer that the employee can be a problem-solver, as well as having adequate confidence when speaking to a group of team-members in a meeting.
